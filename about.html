taryn started drawing when she was young, and decided to never stop. her first notebooks were filled with pokemon and sailor moon, and her latest sketchbooks are filled with dogs and cozy home floorplans.



pepper is a good dog. she likes to eat the following things: dog food, blueberries, pumpkins, things she finds in the grass, rocks. she does not have a discerning tongue, nor is she actually dainty.
